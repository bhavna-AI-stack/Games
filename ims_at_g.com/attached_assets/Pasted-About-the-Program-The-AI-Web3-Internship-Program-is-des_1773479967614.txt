About the Program

The AI + Web3 Internship Program is designed for students and developers who want to build a career in blockchain technology and decentralized applications.

This program combines structured learning, practical tasks, and real-world projects to help participants gain industry-ready skills.

During the training program, participants learn essential technologies used in Web3 development including blockchain fundamentals, smart contracts, frontend frameworks, backend systems, and AI integrations.

Internship Selection Process

There are two ways to join the internship program.

Option 1 — Complete Training Program

Participants who complete the entire training program and submit required tasks can directly join the internship.

Option 2 — Pass the Technical Test

Experienced applicants may attempt a technical test. Those who pass can join the internship without completing the full training program.

Internship Work

Selected interns will work on real projects including:

Web3 decentralized applications

Smart contract development

Blockchain security analysis

React frontend development

Node.js backend APIs

AI-powered Web3 tools
